• CHRISTIAN PERRY •

Artist and Educator

Christian Perry has traveled the U.S. performing in the national tours of JM Barrie's Peter Pan and Marth Speaks, based on the children's book and PBS series. He was in the Off-Broadway production of Skippyjon Jones Snow What at the Lucille Lortel Theatre - a performance The New York Times called "brilliant". Christian is an advocate for early literacy and has led professional development workshops for teachers and families. He works as a Community Educator with the Children's Museum of Manhattan.
